About Me

Elena Voss is a passionate digital artist and concept artist working remotely. With over 8 years of experience, she specializes in creating immersive fantasy and sci-fi visuals for games, books, and personal projects.

Her style blends vibrant colors, intricate details, and atmospheric lighting to bring imaginative worlds to life. Inspired by nature, mythology, and cyberpunk aesthetics, Elena's work has been featured in independent games and online galleries.

She is proficient in industry-standard tools and always exploring new techniques to push creative boundaries. Elena is available for freelance commissions, concept work, and collaborations.
